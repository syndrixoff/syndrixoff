# SYNDRIX Brand Identity Guidelines

## Brand Overview

### Brand Name
SYNDRIX

### Tagline
Engineered with Intelligence

### Industry
Custom Electronics Engineering & Hardware Solutions

### Positioning
SYNDRIX is an engineering-driven company specializing in custom electronic systems, embedded technologies, product development, PCB design, and intelligent hardware solutions. We bridge the gap between innovative ideas and reliable real-world products through precision engineering and intelligent problem-solving.

## Brand Foundation

### Mission
To transform ideas into reliable electronic systems through intelligent engineering and precision execution.

### Vision
To become a trusted engineering partner for innovators, startups, research organizations, and industries seeking advanced electronic solutions.

### Core Values
- **Precision** — Every design decision is based on accuracy, engineering rigor, and technical excellence.
- **Intelligence** — We solve complex challenges through thoughtful engineering and innovation.
- **Reliability** — Products and systems are designed to perform consistently in real-world environments.
- **Innovation** — We continuously explore new technologies and smarter approaches.
- **Partnership** — We work closely with clients throughout the development journey.

## Color Palette

| Color | HEX | Purpose | Meaning |
|-------|-----|---------|---------|
| **Obsidian Black** | `#111317` | Primary brand color | Authority, sophistication, precision, engineering confidence |
| **Deep Cobalt** | `#1D4ED8` | Primary technology color | Trust, intelligence, technical expertise, reliability |
| **Electric Cyan** | `#00D4FF` | Innovation accent | Electronics, digital systems, signal transmission, innovation |
| **Titanium Gray** | `#8E99A8` | Supporting neutral | Industrial engineering, metal components, balance |
| **Arctic White** | `#F8FAFC` | Background color | Clarity, openness, precision |

### Usage
- **Obsidian Black** — Logo, headlines, documentation, packaging
- **Deep Cobalt** — UI elements, key brand accents, website highlights
- **Electric Cyan** — Highlights, product visuals, feature emphasis (max 10% of design)
- **Titanium Gray** — Secondary text, dividers, technical graphics
- **Arctic White** — Documents, presentations, website backgrounds

## Graphic Motif

### The SYNDRIX Signal Path
A recurring circuit-inspired line pattern representing:
- Data flow
- Problem solving
- Engineering pathways
- System connectivity

This motif should appear consistently across all brand materials.
